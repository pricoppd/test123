In the context of Azure Blob Storage, "blob lease break" refers to the action of forcefully breaking a lease on a blob. A blob lease is a mechanism that allows you to lock a blob to prevent other operations from modifying or deleting it. Blob leases are often used to implement scenarios such as distributed coordination, ensuring exclusive access to a blob, or preventing accidental modifications.
